Eliezer E. Wong

An Enthusiastic Life-long Learner and Avid Traveller

Bio

Eliezer was born in Tulsa, Oklahoma, and raised in Singapore. He graduated with special distinction in Dec 2021 from the University of Oklahoma at Norman with a degree in International Business and Management Information Systems. Throughout his academic career at OU, he has managed to maintain a cumulative GPA of 4.0 and has been recognized for his academic achievement in a special award ceremony.

Beyond his academic accomplishments, Eliezer is also passionate about experiencing new cultures from his travels. He has been to numerous countries all over Asia which includes: Singapore, Malaysia, Vietnam, Thailand, Taiwan, Japan, China, and Indonesia. His inquisitiveness and adaptability allows him to embrace change and competently function in uncertain circumstances.

Furthermore, Eliezer also served in the Republic of Singapore Airforce for 2 years. During his conscription, he topped his company in terms of performance and is held in high regard by his peers. As such, he was given the unique opportunity to train as a military pilot and has flown in the CT4B areobatic single prop plane in Tamworth, Australia.
